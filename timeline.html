Magdalene's story is being told in art, music, and books. More so every day. Sophie's grandmother plainly states that even if the actual Grail remains hidden, its truth will be expressed in many art forms. She openly connects modern art with the earlier art of Leonardo da Vinci (and others) as avenues to revealing the Grail's truth.

— Marie Chauvel, Chapter 105
